WANT TO INVEST IN PROPERTY?

If you want to invest in your own residential real estate property, you have to be sure you do the right research. It is imperative to speak with an accountant, an attorney, and a broker to make sure you understand the purchase process, the legal implications, and the financial implications. Additionally, it is highly recommended you have surplus of liquid assets to finance any large repairs that need to occur on the property. Though sad, many investors choose to as little money into their investment as possible. This is results in property degradation and at times a worse tax situation.
